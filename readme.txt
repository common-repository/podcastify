Podcastify helps to host and display Series and Episode on WordPress. And further it generates the feed url to show Podcasts on Popular Podcasting platforms such as Apple Podcasts and Google Podcasts.
== Description ==

Podcastify is a WordPress dedicated Plugin for Podcast users. Podcastify helps to convert your simple website into fully functional Podcasting website.
It helps to host the Podcasts Episodes and Podcast series directly on WordPress and also generates the feeds that helps to integrate podcasts with Apple Podcasts,
Google Podcasts, Spotify, and other popular podcasting platforms. 

**Top Features**

- User Friendly Dashboard.
- Elegant and Responsive Player.
- Easy to create Series and Episodes from WordPress Website.
- Use Feed url given by Podcastify to show your Podcasts On Apple Podcasts, Google Podcasts, Spotify and other popular Podcasting platforms.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==


= How to host my podcasts ?=

  You can either host your Podcasts on other servers or you can directly upload podcasts on your WordPress website. If you are hosting on third party server you need to add url in WordPress Podcast Episode's Dashboard.


= Does Podcastify provides with Player?=

  Yes, Podcastify gives a Responsive player that can be matched with your current theme.
